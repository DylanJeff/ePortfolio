Web Design and Development

In Web Design and Development (WDD) I have learned how to create a website from scratch using HTML and CSS.

What I enjoy about WDD:

  1. Lots of code
  2. Get to build something from the ground up
  3. Brand new skills learned in CSS
  4. Learn techniques and practises that are used in professional work

My future in WDD will involve me learning about JavaScript integration and the uses of servers through PHP and MySQL.

My opinion of WDD

Web Design and Development is one of the modules I enjoy the most. Being a fan of coding, I feel it is a natural part of the course for me. I'm really liking it so far, by looking at whats left in the year I think this will continue. The module overall is taught really well, with challenging labs and lectures that actually keep you interested.

Computer Systems Fundamentals

In Computer Systems Fundamentals (CSF) I have learned about how a Computer is a barebones logic machine built with miniscule components.

What I enjoy about CSF:

  1. Put purpose to already known content
  2. Answered some questions I may have had before coming to University
  3. Working with logic
  4. Learning a decent bit of Maths aswell

My future in CSF will involve me learning more and more maths related to Computers. I'm not complaining, I like Maths.

My opinion of CSF

Computer Systems Fundamentals has been the most interesting module for me so far, as it has introduced the most new things to me. I didn't really think I was intrigued as much when it come down to the depths of a computer, but I have found myself pleasantly shocked. The lectures are a highlight of this course for me as I think they are performed at a perfect pace, and are full of content for making good notes with. The labs are also a great credit with exercises that don't only reinforce your understanding but expand it.
