Information Systems

In Information Systems (IS) I have learned about databases, the planning of them and their uses in business.

What I enjoy about IS:

  1. Insight into Computer Science in business
  2. Gives me more understanding of why CS is so important
  3. Probably the most useful in terms of post University skills
  4. Get to organise stuff

My future in IS will involve me continuing the assignment of designing and developing a database for a fictional small business.

My opinion of IS

Information Systems is a balanced module for me. I do enjoy designing, creating and organising databases. Yet on the other hand it isn't my preferred activity. Databases and IS in general tends to be more focused on the business side of things, whereas I'm more technical. Of course I understand that in an ever digitalising world, databases are the core of everything worth something nowadays and that if theres anything you need to learn for employability purposes, its IS.
